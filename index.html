ABOUT US

Our company Michranki Cool Ltd is mainly focused in the field of refrigeration, electromechanical, ventilation, hotel and professional kitchen equipment since 2003. It was founded by Michalis Michael, after 22 years of experience in this field, in Larnaca.

With our specialized workshops we are offering to our customers space study, design, configuration, installation, maintenance and repairing.

We provide new and used equipment as well as we undertake the design and installation of cold rooms according to customer needs.
